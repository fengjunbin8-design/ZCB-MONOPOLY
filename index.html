Game Rules

[ BACKGROUND / 遊戲背景 ]

Based on Hong Kong's CIC Zero Carbon Park, this game simulates the decision-making process of different stakeholders in ecological construction, allowing players to understand zero-carbon design strategies, the balance between ecology and urban development, and the differences in the positions of different stakeholders. 本遊戲以香港零碳天地為原型，通過模擬不同利益相關者在生態建設中的決策過程，讓玩家理解零碳設計策略、生態與城市發展的平衡、不同利益相關方的立場差異。

[ ROLES / 角色設定 ]

[CIC] Construction Industry Council (建造業議會)
They hope to promote construction efficiency and technology implementation, and prefer to invest in large-scale projects. 希望能推動建設效率與技術落地，更偏好投資大型項目（如展館、技術設施）。

[NGO] Environmental NGO (生態保護組織)
With the goal of maximizing ecological value, priority is given to the development of ecological spaces such as wetlands. 以實現最大化生態價值為目標，優先建設濕地、原生林等生態空間。

[PUB] The Public (公眾)
They hope to improve the public experience and fairness, and prefer education, exhibitions, and public spaces. 希望能提升公共體驗與公平性，偏好教育、展覽、公共空間。

[ CORE MECHANICS / 基礎規則 ]

  • Initialization: Each group starts with $500. The game features a "Dual Token Mode", where each group has two pieces to manage. 初始與行動: 每組初始資金為 $500。遊戲採用「雙棋子模式」，每組擁有兩枚棋子。玩家自行選擇移動 [1] 號或 [2] 號棋子。
  • Pass GO: Whenever any token passes the "START GO" tile, that faction receives a $200 stipend. 經過起點: 任何一枚棋子在移動中經過「起點 GO」，該陣營將獲得 $200 獎勵。
  • Investment: Construction cost is a flat $100. Each group can only build plots that match their goals. 投資建設: 每格建造費用固定為 $100，每組只能購買符合自身陣營目標的地塊。
  • Pay Toll: If you stay on a plot built by another group, you must pay a visitation fee. Rent levels: Lv.1=$100, Lv.2=$200, Lv.3=$300. 支付參觀費: 停留他人已建地塊需支付費用。1級地塊$100、2級地塊$200、3級地塊$300。

[ SPECIAL TILES / 特殊地塊 ]

  • CHANCE (機會卡): 抽取機會卡，執行隨機事件。
  • QUIZ (生態問答): 回答正確獲得 $200，錯誤失去 $200。
  • FOUNDATION (基金贊助): 獲得綠色建設基金贊助 $400。
  • WARNING (惡劣天氣): 遭遇颱風暴雨，本回合行動結束。
  • FAST TRAVEL (自由選擇): 可將行動棋子自由移動到地圖上的任意位置。
